Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoloSpin. This Privacy Policy explains how The Transcendence Team ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data required to provide the game experience:

  • Authentication Data: When you log in via Google or GitHub, we collect your Email Address and Username. Note that we do not fetch your profile picture from these providers; you are assigned a default avatar upon registration.
  • User-Generated Content:
    • Avatars: If you choose to upload a custom profile picture, we store this image on our servers.
    • Chat Logs: We store messages sent between you and your friends to provide chat history.
  • Game Statistics: We automatically track gameplay data, including Wins, Losses, Scores, Goals Scored/Conceded, Experience Points (XP), and Level.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To Identify You: We use your OAuth credentials to create and secure your unique account.
  • To Facilitate Gameplay: Your stats (Level, Wins/Losses) are displayed on public leaderboards and match history to enable competitive play.
  • To Enable Communication: Chat messages are stored to allow asynchronous communication between friends.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Chat History: Chat messages are stored permanently while a friendship exists. If either user removes the other as a friend, the chat history between those two users is permanently deleted.
  • Account Deletion: You may request the full deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at midbella@student.1337.ma.

5. Cookies

We use Strictly Necessary Cookies only.

  • JWT (JSON Web Token): We store a secure, HTTP-Only cookie to maintain your logged-in session. We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.
